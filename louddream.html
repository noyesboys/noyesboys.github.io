About Loud Dreams

Loud Dreams represents the pinnacle of sativa breeding, combining high-grade Haze genetics with exceptional lemon selections to create a strain that delivers both incredible potency and unmatched citrus terpene expression. This sativa-dominant powerhouse showcases the energetic, cerebral effects that made classic sativas legendary while adding modern breeding improvements for consistent performance and exceptional yields.

The strain exhibits classic sativa structure with tall growth, excellent stretch, and beautiful elongated colas that develop intense lemon and citrus aromatics throughout flowering. The high CBD content adds therapeutic value while the dominant Limonene terpenes create an uplifting, mood-enhancing experience that's perfect for daytime use and creative activities.

Effects are intensely energizing and motivating, providing clean cerebral stimulation that enhances focus, creativity, and productivity. The exceptional terpene profile delivers loud, unmistakable lemon aromas that translate beautifully to flavor, making every session a bright, uplifting experience. Perfect for users seeking powerful daytime effects with therapeutic benefits.

Flavor Profile

Dominant lemon and citrus flavors with bright, zesty character. Exceptional Limonene content creates loud, unmistakable aromatics that fill the room and deliver clean, energizing effects.

Effects

Intensely energizing and motivating with clear-headed cerebral stimulation. Perfect for creativity, productivity, and active pursuits. High potency delivers long-lasting, functional effects.

Growing

Classic tall sativa structure with excellent stretch and yield potential. Thrives in both indoor and outdoor environments with proper support. Beautiful elongated colas with exceptional resin production.

Therapeutic Value

Higher CBD content adds therapeutic benefits while maintaining energizing effects. Excellent for mood disorders, fatigue, and daytime pain management without sedation.
