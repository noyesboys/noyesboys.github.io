HOW BREEDING WORKS

Purchase a Male Plant (♂) from the shop for 5,000¢. Plant it in the same room as flowering females. On Day 20 of flower the male begins dropping pollen — females in the room will start forming seeds from Week 6 of flower onwards. Harvest the seeded female to collect seeds. Name your new strain and it's registered here. Each generation you breed moves toward stability — reaching F10 gives a 90% GOD chance on every pop.

✂️ TRAIN PLANT
Apply training techniques to improve yield and shape. Some methods are irreversible.
✂️ TOP — Remove apical tip
Creates 2 main colas instead of 1. Best done in early veg. Can be done twice (max). Each top adds a topped sprite layer.
🔗 LST — Low Stress Training
Bend and tie branches to open the canopy. Increases light penetration. Reversible, slow yield benefit.
3
🌿 FIM — Pinch & Bend
Less precise than topping — creates 3–5 new growth points. More chaotic canopy, high yield potential.
🍂 DEFOLIATE — Remove fan leaves
Removes large fan leaves blocking bud sites. Only recommended in week 3+ of flower. Risk: if overdone, reduces health.
🕸️ SCROG — Screen of Green
Weave branches through a screen for even canopy. Requires SCROG net (shop). Big yield bonus if done right.
