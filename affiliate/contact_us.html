Yes! All of our seeds are feminized, ensuring you get female plants that produce the flowers you're looking for. Our feminization process achieves 99%+ female rates.

Absolutely! We're passionate growers ourselves and love helping customers succeed. Contact us with any growing questions or check out our growing guide for detailed information.
