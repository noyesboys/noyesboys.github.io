About Afghani Noyes

Afghani Noyes represents the marriage of ancient landrace genetics with modern Noyes Boys breeding excellence. This heavily indica-dominant strain combines the legendary Afghani landrace with the signature Noyes Kush genetics, creating a powerhouse indica that delivers both traditional hash-plant characteristics and contemporary potency levels. The result is a strain that honors cannabis heritage while meeting modern quality standards.

The strain expresses classic Afghani characteristics with broad, dark green leaves and extremely resinous buds that develop deep purple hues in cooler temperatures. Plants grow with the characteristic short, bushy Afghani structure while incorporating the enhanced resin production and potency that Noyes genetics are known for. The terpene profile features earthy, hashy, and spicy notes with subtle sweet undertones that create complex, traditional flavors.

Effects are quintessentially indica with deep body relaxation, sedative qualities, and powerful pain relief properties. The high begins with gentle mental euphoria that quickly transitions into full-body stone perfect for evening use and medical applications. This strain excels at treating chronic pain, insomnia, anxiety, and appetite issues. The exceptional resin production makes it outstanding for traditional hash making, maintaining the authentic flavors and potent effects that made Afghani genetics legendary.
