About Citrus Haze Breath

Citrus Haze Breath represents an innovative approach to hybrid breeding, combining the energizing, citrus-forward characteristics of premium Haze genetics with the relaxing, therapeutic qualities of Mendo Breath. This sativa-dominant hybrid showcases Noyes Boys' expertise in balancing contrasting genetics to create a strain that offers both mental stimulation and physical relaxation.

The strain exhibits the classic sativa structure with tall growth, elongated internodes, and beautiful light-green foliage that develops stunning citrus aromas during flowering. The Breath genetics contribute to enhanced resin production and add a creamy, vanilla undertone that complements the bright citrus flavors. This combination creates a complex terpene profile that's both invigorating and soothing.

Effects are perfectly balanced for daytime use - the initial cerebral uplift provides energy, focus, and creativity, while the Breath genetics ensure a smooth, relaxing comedown without sedation. This makes Citrus Haze Breath ideal for productive activities, social gatherings, or creative endeavors where you want mental clarity with a touch of physical comfort.

🍊 Flavor Profile

Bright citrus and lemon zest with creamy vanilla undertones from Breath genetics. Dominant Limonene and Valencene create refreshing, uplifting aromatics with smooth finish.

⚡ Effects

Energizing cerebral uplift with gentle body relaxation. Perfect for daytime productivity, creative work, and social activities. Balanced high that doesn't overwhelm.
