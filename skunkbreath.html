About Skunk Breath

Skunk Breath combines the legendary pungency of classic Skunk genetics with the deeply relaxing qualities of Noyes Breath, creating an indica-dominant powerhouse that delivers both nostalgic flavors and modern potency. This F1 hybrid showcases the best of both parent strains - the unmistakable skunky, gassy aroma that made Skunk genetics famous worldwide, enhanced by the sedative breath characteristics that provide ultimate evening relaxation.

The strain develops with classic indica structure, staying short to medium height while producing dense, resinous buds covered in a thick layer of trichomes. The terpene profile is dominated by humulene and myrcene, creating complex layers of skunky, gassy, and rubber-like aromas that transport users back to the golden age of cannabis. Plants are easy to manage indoors with their compact structure and reliable 64-day flowering cycle.

Effects are heavily indica-leaning with powerful sedative qualities perfect for evening use and sleep disorders. The high begins with gentle euphoria and happiness that quickly transitions into deep body relaxation and sleepiness. At 28% THC, this strain packs serious potency while maintaining the smooth, comfortable stone that breath genetics are known for. Medical users appreciate its effectiveness for insomnia, chronic pain, anxiety, and appetite stimulation.
