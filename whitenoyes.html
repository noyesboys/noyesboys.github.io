About White Noyes

White Noyes represents the fusion of premium white genetics with the legendary Noyes Kush backbone, creating an indica-dominant hybrid that showcases stunning white coloration while maintaining the potent effects and reliable growing characteristics that made Noyes genetics famous. This strain produces buds so white they appear almost albino, covered in an exceptional layer of crystal trichomes that create a snow-like appearance unlike any other strain.

The strain develops its signature white coloration naturally throughout flowering, with buds that range from pure white to pale green with heavy crystal coverage. Plants grow with medium height and robust structure, producing dense, resinous flowers that are both visually stunning and incredibly potent. The terpene profile combines the earthy, gassy notes of Noyes Kush with subtle sweet and pine undertones that complement the strain's unique appearance.

Effects lean indica-dominant with deeply relaxing body effects balanced by uplifting mental clarity. The high begins with gentle cerebral euphoria that transitions into full-body relaxation perfect for evening use or stress relief. Medical users appreciate the strain's effectiveness for pain management, anxiety, and sleep disorders. The exceptional trichome production makes White Noyes outstanding for concentrate production, yielding high-quality extracts that maintain the strain's unique characteristics and potent effects.
