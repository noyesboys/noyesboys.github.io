About Captain Noyes

Captain Noyes commands respect with its powerful combination of Noyes Kush foundation genetics and legendary Trainwreck energy. This strain navigates users toward motivated, energetic highs while maintaining the reliability and consistency that Noyes genetics are known for. Despite being indica-leaning at 75%, the Trainwreck influence ensures uplifting, productive effects.

The strain showcases classic gas, earthy, and citrus terpenes dominated by humulene and limonene, creating a familiar yet distinctive aroma profile. Plants maintain the compact, manageable structure of Noyes genetics while expressing the vigor and resilience inherited from Trainwreck. The 65-day flowering time allows for proper development without extended waiting periods.

Captain Noyes steers users toward happy, energetic, and motivated states, making it excellent for daytime productivity, creative projects, and social activities. The moderate THC levels provide effective results without overwhelming intensity, while the slight CBD content adds therapeutic balance. This strain represents the perfect fusion of foundation genetics with legendary sativa energy.
