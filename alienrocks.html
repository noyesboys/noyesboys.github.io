About Alien Rocks

Alien Rocks represents a unique entry in the Noyes Boys Geode Collection, where otherworldly genetics meet the earth-bound stability that defines the Noyes breeding program. This indica-dominant hybrid delivers an exotic flavor profile combining grape sweetness with gassy fuel notes, earthy undertones, and bright citrus accents - creating a complex terpene bouquet dominated by humulene and limonene that's truly out of this world.

The strain develops beautiful purple and green coloration throughout flowering, with dense, rock-hard buds covered in an otherworldly blanket of sparkling trichomes that give it an almost extraterrestrial appearance. The Geode genetics provide the structural foundation and reliability, while the exotic parent genetics contribute unique flavors and effects that set Alien Rocks apart from more traditional offerings.

Effects are perfectly balanced between cerebral exploration and physical relaxation - the sativa influence provides creative, uplifting mental effects while the indica dominance ensures deep body relaxation without overwhelming sedation. Perfect for evening creativity sessions, social gatherings, or exploring new dimensions of relaxation. The unique flavor profile and balanced effects make Alien Rocks a standout choice for cannabis connoisseurs seeking something truly different in their collection.
