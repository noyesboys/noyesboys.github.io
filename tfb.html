About TFB

TFB represents a powerful fusion of legendary Alaskan Thunder genetics with the deeply relaxing Noyes Breath lineage. This indica-dominant hybrid delivers maximum potency at 28% THC while maintaining the manageable effects and therapeutic benefits that make it perfect for evening relaxation and sleep aid.

The strain combines the robust growth characteristics and resin production of Alaskan Thunder with the sedative, body-focused effects of Noyes Breath. Plants finish in a quick 62 days while producing exceptional yields both indoors and outdoors. The short to medium height makes this strain suitable for various growing environments.

Effects are powerfully relaxing and sedative, making TFB ideal for users seeking deep stress relief, pain management, and sleep aid. The high THC content provides potent therapeutic benefits while the indica dominance ensures a calming, body-focused experience. The combination of gas and rubber terpenes creates a distinctive aroma profile that's both pungent and appealing.
