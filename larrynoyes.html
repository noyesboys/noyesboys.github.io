About Larry Noyes

Larry Noyes is an exceptional sativa-dominant hybrid that combines the legendary Larry OG with Noyes Boys Genetics' signature Noyes Kush. At 90% sativa, this strain delivers pure energizing effects while maintaining the quality OG characteristics that made Larry OG famous. The cross creates a unique expression that leans heavily sativa in effects and growth pattern while retaining the potency and bag appeal of both parent strains.

Plants grow tall with classic sativa structure - long internodal spacing, thin leaves, and impressive stretch during flowering. The Larry OG influence provides that iconic lime-green coloration and dense, resinous buds despite the sativa dominance. The longer flowering time of 70-75 days allows full terpene and cannabinoid development, resulting in complex flavors mixing earthy OG notes with bright, uplifting sativa characteristics.

Effects are powerfully cerebral and energizing - perfect for daytime use, creative projects, or active pursuits. Users report clear-headed focus, motivation, and sustained energy without anxiety or jitters. The high sativa content provides mental clarity and creativity while the OG genetics add depth and quality to the experience. This strain is ideal for those seeking pure sativa effects with the legitimacy and potency of OG lineage.
