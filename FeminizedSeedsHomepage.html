The Noyes Boys Story

Founded with a passion for cannabis genetics and a commitment to quality, The Noyes Boys Seed Bank has been serving Canadian growers since 2015. We believe that great cannabis starts with exceptional genetics.

Our breeding program focuses on plant structure and resilience first, knowing that potency and flavor naturally follow when you work with the right genetics. Every strain in our collection has been carefully selected and bred for Canadian growing conditions.

We breed for Canadian Purples, dense but well-spaced internoding, mildew resistance, and ease of growth. Our goal is to provide genetics that perform consistently, whether you're a first-time grower or a seasoned cultivator.
