About Citrus Squirts

Citrus Squirts is a heavily sativa-dominant hybrid that delivers an explosive burst of citrus flavors with energizing effects perfect for daytime use. This strain combines the legendary Alaskan Thunder Fuck (ATF) genetics with the citrus-forward Citronoyes Haze to create a powerhouse sativa that tastes like fresh-squeezed oranges and provides long-lasting motivation and creativity.

The flavor profile is dominated by bright citrus, fruity, and orange notes that literally burst on the palate - hence the name "Squirts." The terpene profile features high levels of limonene and valencene, creating layers of citrus complexity with subtle earthy undertones. Plants grow tall and stretchy with classic sativa structure, producing large, airy buds covered in bright orange pistils that match the strain's citrus profile.

Effects are distinctly sativa-leaning with uplifting cerebral stimulation, enhanced creativity, and sustained energy. Users report feeling relaxed yet happy and hungry, making this strain perfect for social situations, creative projects, and daytime activities. The longer flowering time is typical of sativa genetics but rewards growers with exceptional yields and premium quality flower that commands attention.
