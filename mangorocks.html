About Mango Rocks

Mango Rocks represents the perfect fusion of tropical mango genetics with rock-solid foundation genetics from the Noyes Boys breeding program. This Geode Collection strain embodies the "sparkling resin production meets rock-solid genetics" philosophy, delivering exceptional bag appeal with the reliability and potency that serious growers demand.

The strain showcases the characteristic short, compact structure that made the Geode Collection famous, with dense internoding and heavy bud development that creates rock-like density. The complex terpene profile features Linalool for tropical floral notes, Humulene for earthy depth, and Limonene for bright mango and citrus characteristics that create an exotic flavor experience.

Effects combine the motivating, energetic characteristics typical of premium genetics with the happy, productive buzz that makes this strain perfect for daytime use. The rock-solid genetics ensure consistent performance while the tropical mango influence adds an exotic twist that sets this strain apart from typical offerings.

Flavor Profile

Dominant tropical mango flavors with gas, earthy, and citrus undertones. Complex terpene profile featuring Linalool, Humulene, and Limonene creates layered tropical experience.

Rock-Solid Genetics

Geode Collection breeding ensures consistent performance with sparkling resin production and reliable structure. Dense, rock-like buds with exceptional bag appeal and potency.

Short, compact plants perfect for indoor cultivation. Dense internoding with heavy bud development. Excellent resin production and manageable structure throughout flowering.

Effects
