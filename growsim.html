How It Works

  1. Plant seeds of your chosen strain.
  2. Adjust environment (light, water, nutrients, and temperature).
  3. Watch your plants grow and face random challenges (pests, mildew).
  4. Harvest the crop, analyze potency/yield, and sell or upgrade your equipment.
