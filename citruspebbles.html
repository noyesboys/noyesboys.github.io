About Citrus Pebbles

Citrus Pebbles combines the explosive citrus flavors of premium Citrus genetics with the dense, pebble-like structure that made Noyes Boys Genetics famous. This sativa-leaning hybrid produces small, incredibly dense buds that burst with intense lemon, orange, and grapefruit flavors while maintaining the compact, space-efficient growing characteristics that growers love. Each nug resembles a citrus-colored pebble covered in sparkling trichomes that emit powerful citrus aromas.

The strain's unique structure maximizes citrus terpene concentration in compact form, creating an incredibly potent flavor experience. The terpene profile is dominated by limonene and humulene, producing complex layers of zesty citrus with gas and earthy undertones. Plants grow with manageable height while producing remarkably dense flowers that pack maximum potency into minimal space, making this strain perfect for both commercial and home cultivation.

Effects lean slightly sativa with uplifting cerebral euphoria balanced by gentle body relaxation. The high begins with an energizing citrus-flavored rush that promotes creativity and social interaction, then transitions into balanced hybrid effects perfect for any time of day. The intense terpene profile makes this strain exceptional for both flower consumption and concentrate production, with hash and rosin maintaining the explosive citrus character in concentrated form.
