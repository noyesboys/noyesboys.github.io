The Noyes Kush Origin Story

A blend of at least 10+ strains developed over 10+ years of passionate hunting, crossing, and refinement

NOYES KUSH (F8)

NOT for sale at this time

Our flagship genetic - a complex blend of at least 10+ strains that represents over a decade of careful selection, testing, and refinement. This is our "stud" (reversed female) that forms the backbone of many of our current offerings.

The Beginning - Genetic Lineage Explanation

Noyes Kush is a blend of at least 10+ strains, developed over 10+ years of hunting. Even we didn't know exactly what some of them were sometimes - we just knew they were fire! Think seeds found in primo nugs or equivalents, like washed away labels in storage.

The Early Days - Learning and Discovery

We never planned to sell genetics back then and were just popping beans and learning. Hidden through the trash, we started to find all sorts of gem traits, and felt the best way to save and share them with friends would be to start making seeds.

The Crossing Experiments

A year or two in, we decided to take a hand at crossing some of the genetic oddities that we liked with hardier strains. We have thrown them together to see what sticks, what's trash, and what's worth keeping. We test it, stress test it, and taste it, then throw another genetic at it and decide if that blend is better or worse through testing, and then progress.

The Beautiful Chaos

It's a bit of a cluster, yes, but for a personal project that was never meant to be more than shared, we've seen some beautiful phenos from our final cross and have back-crossed 3x for stability. At the end of the day, we still don't know "exactly" what it is per say, but we know what stuck to make this pheno so fire.

Our Breeding Philosophy

Structure, resilience, and ease of growth - everything else follows naturally

We breed for Canadian purples, dense, short plants with well-spaced internoding, all while looking for that perfect smell, profile and taste.

Structure Over Everything

High THC and good flavour is getting common these days, so we decided to breed for proper plant structure, mildew resistance and ease of growth for beginners. Plant structure and resilience will always be our foremost goal over just simply high THC or good smell - those should just come naturally if you're working with the right genetics... like ours.

Our focus on structure, resilience, and ease of growth ensures that both novice and experienced growers can achieve success with our genetics. We believe that when you start with solid genetics, everything else - potency, flavor, and bag appeal - follows naturally.
