About Hashfingers

Hashfingers represents the pinnacle of hash-making genetics, bred specifically for extract artists and concentrate enthusiasts who demand maximum resin production and premium hash quality. This strain combines the legendary Hash Plant genetics with the ultra-sticky characteristics of Sticky Fingers, creating a cultivar that literally coats your fingers in golden resin during handling.

The name says it all - Hashfingers produces such abundant, sticky trichomes that trimming and handling the buds results in fingers completely covered in premium finger hash. This isn't just about quantity; the quality of the resin is exceptional, with complex terpene profiles and potent cannabinoid content that creates world-class hash and concentrates.

Effects provide the perfect balance for hash enthusiasts - clear-headed enough for concentrate work but potent enough to deliver serious relief. The 60/40 indica-dominant profile offers relaxing body effects while maintaining mental clarity, making it ideal for both medicinal users and those who appreciate craft hash traditions. This strain bridges old-school hash culture with modern concentrate innovation.
