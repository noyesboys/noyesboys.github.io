About Yesno Kush

Yesno Kush represents the ultimate genetic experiment - a "genetic lottery" created by combining pollen from all 5 of the original Line 1 strains and applying it to a second batch of the same 5 Line 1 strains. This creates an incredibly diverse F2 population with unlimited phenotypic variation, making every seed a unique genetic adventure and every plant a potential treasure hunt for rare traits and novel expressions.

The name "Yesno" perfectly captures the experimental nature of this strain - you never know what you're going to get until you grow it out. Each seed carries genetic potential from Purple Rainbow, Rainbow Cakes, Cake Breath, Alienoyes Kush, Mangonoyes Kush, Citronoyes Haze, Noyes Haze, Noyes Breath, Meat Cakes, and Biscotti Rocks. The genetic combinations are virtually limitless, creating opportunities to discover completely new phenotypes and expressions.

This strain is specifically designed for phenotype hunting and experimentation rather than commercial production. Growers should expect extreme variation in height, structure, flowering time, potency, flavor profiles, and effects. Some plants may express sativa-dominant traits with energizing effects and citrus flavors, while others may show indica-dominant characteristics with sedative properties and gassy aromas. The goal is exploration and discovery of unique genetic expressions that could become future breeding stock.
