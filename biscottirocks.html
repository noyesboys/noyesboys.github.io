About Biscotti Rocks

Biscotti Rocks represents the pinnacle of the Noyes Boys "Geode" breeding line, combining the beloved cookies and cream flavors of Biscotti with the rock-hard density and structural improvements that define the Geode genetics series. This heavily indica-dominated strain produces some of the densest, most compact flowers available while maintaining the sweet, nutty flavors that make Biscotti genetics so sought after.

Named for its incredibly dense, Rockle-like buds that are as hard as stones, this strain showcases exceptional trichome production with a crystalline appearance reminiscent of crushed diamonds. The plant structure is exceptionally robust with thick stems, tight internodal spacing, and branches that can support massive weight without requiring support stakes - a hallmark of the Geode line's structural improvements.

The effects combine the best of both worlds - the sweet, relaxing stone of Biscotti genetics with the profound body effects that indica-dominant strains are known for. Perfect for evening use, pain management, and those seeking powerful, long-lasting relief. The combination of premium bag appeal and exceptional potency makes this a standout strain for both medical and recreational users.
