About Stink

Stink lives up to its name with an incredibly pungent aroma that combines classic skunk genetics with the resinous power of hashplant. This heavily indica-dominant strain delivers knockout sedative effects that make it perfect for end-of-day relaxation and sleep aid. The dense, compact buds are loaded with trichomes and emit an intense skunky, gassy, and cheesy aroma that can fill an entire room.

The terpene profile is dominated by humulene and myrcene, creating the signature skunk funk with earthy, gassy undertones and hints of aged cheese. Plants grow with a short to medium stature, making them ideal for indoor cultivation and SOG setups. The hashplant genetics contribute to exceptional resin production and the dense, rock-hard bud structure that serious indica lovers crave.

Effects are powerfully sedating with deep body relaxation that can lead to couch lock and eventual sleep. The heavy indica influence makes this strain perfect for treating insomnia, chronic pain, muscle spasms, and stress. Users report feeling deeply relaxed and tired, with a euphoric mental state that gradually fades into peaceful sedation. This is old-school indica power at its finest.
