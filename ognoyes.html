About OG Noyes

OG Noyes represents the perfect marriage of legendary OG Kush genetics with the robust Canadian breeding of Noyes Kush. This indica-dominant powerhouse combines the classic fuel-forward flavors and potent stone of OG with the enhanced structure, mildew resistance, and yield improvements that make Noyes Boys genetics so sought after.

Bred specifically to bring classic OG genetics into the Canadian climate, OG Noyes maintains all the beloved characteristics of traditional OG strains while adding the reliability and vigor needed for consistent outdoor and greenhouse cultivation. The Noyes Kush influence strengthens the plant structure, increases resin production, and provides exceptional resistance to common cannabis ailments.

The effects are pure OG bliss - a powerful cerebral rush followed by a deeply relaxing body stone that's perfect for evening use. This strain delivers the authentic OG experience that connoisseurs crave while offering the dependable performance that commercial growers demand. It's old-school flavor meets new-school reliability.
