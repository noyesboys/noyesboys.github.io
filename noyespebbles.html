About Noyes Pebbles

Noyes Pebbles represents the compact evolution of the legendary Noyes Kush genetics, specifically bred for growers who need maximum potency and quality in minimal space. This indica-dominant strain produces incredibly dense, pebble-sized buds that pack the full power of Noyes genetics into perfectly compact packages. Despite the smaller stature, Noyes Pebbles delivers exceptional potency, outstanding bag appeal, and the rock-solid reliability that defines the Noyes Boys breeding program.

The strain was developed through careful selection of the most compact Noyes Kush phenotypes, creating a plant that stays short and bushy while maintaining all the premium qualities that made the original famous. Each bud is incredibly dense and resinous, resembling little polished stones covered in sparkling trichomes. The compact size makes it perfect for stealth growing, small spaces, or maximizing plant counts in limited areas.

Effects deliver the classic Noyes experience in concentrated form - deeply relaxing indica stone with therapeutic benefits for pain relief, stress reduction, and sleep enhancement. The smaller plant size doesn't compromise potency or quality, making Noyes Pebbles ideal for medical patients with space constraints, urban growers, or anyone seeking premium genetics in a compact package that's easy to manage and highly discreet.
