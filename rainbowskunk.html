About Rainbow Skunk

Rainbow Skunk is a stunning indica-dominant hybrid that combines the classic pungent appeal of Skunk genetics with vibrant, colorful characteristics. This strain showcases the Noyes Boys' dedication to breeding beautiful, high-performing plants that thrive in Canadian conditions.

With its stretchy growth pattern and heavy nug development, Rainbow Skunk produces impressive yields both indoors and outdoors. The plant develops beautiful colors throughout flowering while maintaining the robust structure and mildew resistance that Noyes Boys genetics are known for.

The effects are energetic and motivating, making this an excellent choice for daytime use or creative activities. Despite its indica dominance, the sativa influence provides an uplifting cerebral buzz that keeps you focused and happy.

🌈 Flavor Profile

Skunky and gassy base notes with bright berry undertones. The terpene profile features Humulene for earthy, gassy flavors and Limonene for citrus brightness.

🎯 Effects

Energetic, happy, and motivated. Perfect for staying productive while enjoying a pleasant cerebral buzz. Great for creativity and social activities.
