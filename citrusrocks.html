About Citrus Rocks

Citrus Rocks represents the pinnacle of citrus genetics combined with rock-solid density and exceptional potency. This sativa-leaning hybrid delivers the explosive citrus flavors of Citronoyes Haze while adding the structural integrity and density that made the "Rocks" series famous. Each bud is literally rock-hard, covered in orange and yellow trichomes that sparkle like citrus-colored gems, creating some of the most visually stunning and potent cannabis available.

The strain produces medium-height plants with exceptional lateral branching, maximizing bud sites while maintaining manageable structure. The terpene profile is dominated by limonene, humulene, and myrcene, creating complex layers of lemon, orange, and grapefruit flavors with subtle gas and earthy undertones. The extended trichome production creates buds so resinous they appear to be coated in citrus-colored crystals, making this strain as visually impressive as it is potent.

Effects lean sativa-dominant with intense cerebral stimulation followed by balanced body relaxation. The high begins with an explosive citrus-flavored rush that provides immediate energy and creativity enhancement, then transitions into sustained focus and motivation perfect for daytime productivity. The rock-hard density and exceptional resin production make this strain outstanding for concentrate production, with rosin and hash maintaining the full spectrum of citrus terpenes in concentrated form.
