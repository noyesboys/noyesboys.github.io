About Le Pew

Le Pew brings together the legendary genetics of Skunk and Trainwreck to create a strain that delivers classic skunky aromas with surprisingly energetic effects. Despite its indica-leaning genetics at 70% indica and 30% sativa, this strain provides motivating and energetic effects that make it perfect for daytime productivity and creative endeavors.

The strain showcases the unmistakable skunky terpene profile dominated by humulene and terpinolene, creating that classic pungent, earthy aroma that skunk genetics are famous for. Plants grow to medium height with robust structure, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. The 63-day flowering time keeps cycles moving efficiently while maintaining quality.

What sets Le Pew apart from typical indica-dominant strains is its energizing effect profile. Users report feelings of motivation, energy, and mental clarity - effects typically associated with sativa strains. This unique combination makes it ideal for those who enjoy the skunky flavor profile but want to remain productive and active throughout their experience.
