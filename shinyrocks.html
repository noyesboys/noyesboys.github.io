About Shiny Rocks

Shiny Rocks lives up to its name with exceptional trichome production that creates a diamond-like sparkle across every surface of the plant. This balanced hybrid combines rock-solid genetics with sparkling resin production, resulting in buds that look like they've been rolled in crushed diamonds. The strain represents the pinnacle of Noyes Boys Genetics' Geode Collection, showcasing how premium genetics can produce both stunning visual appeal and exceptional potency.

The terpene profile is dominated by humulene and limonene, creating complex layers of gas, earthy, and citrus flavors that complement the strain's incredible bag appeal. Plants grow with a medium structure that's manageable for most growing environments while producing dense, crystal-covered buds that are a joy to harvest and cure. The balanced indica/sativa genetics provide versatile effects suitable for various times of day.

Effects offer the best of both worlds - initial cerebral euphoria and creativity from the sativa side, followed by relaxing body effects from the indica genetics. This makes Shiny Rocks perfect for users who want a strain that can transition from productive daytime use to evening relaxation. The high resin content makes this strain exceptional for concentrate production, with hash and rosin maintaining the complex terpene profile.
