About Noyes Thunder

Noyes Thunder combines the legendary power of Alaskan Thunder Fuck with the refined genetics of Noyes Kush to create an indica-dominant hybrid that delivers thunderous effects with incredible flavor complexity. This strain showcases beautiful berry, fruity, and lemony notes that create a sweet and sour flavor profile perfect for those seeking both potent effects and exceptional taste.

The strain expresses a complex terpene profile rich in fruity esters and citrus compounds, creating layers of berry sweetness balanced by bright lemon tartness. Plants grow with a compact, indica structure while maintaining some of the vigor from the ATF genetics. The dense buds often develop purple hues and are covered in a thick layer of resinous trichomes that preserve the intense aromatics.

Effects lean heavily toward the indica side with deeply relaxing body effects combined with an uplifting, energetic mental state. Users report feeling energetic, happy, and motivated initially, followed by a more relaxed body high that makes this strain versatile for both day and evening use. The balanced effects make it excellent for stress relief, mood enhancement, and appetite stimulation while maintaining enough energy to stay functional.

🍓 Berry-Lemon Fusion

Complex flavor profile combining sweet berries with tart lemon notes. Each hit delivers layers of fruity sweetness balanced by bright citrus acidity for a truly memorable taste experience.

⚡ Balanced Energy

Perfect balance of energetic sativa effects with relaxing indica body high. Provides motivation and happiness while maintaining physical comfort and relaxation.

🌱 Compact Powerhouse

🎯 Versatile Effects

Suitable for various times of day and activities. Energetic enough for daytime use but relaxing enough for evening unwinding. Perfect all-around strain.
