About Purple Rainbow

Purple Rainbow represents the pinnacle of Noyes Boys' original purple genetics breeding program. This indica-dominant strain showcases the stunning deep purple coloration that Canadian growers have come to love, combined with rainbow-colored trichomes that create an absolutely mesmerizing visual display.

Bred specifically for Canadian conditions, Purple Rainbow thrives in shorter growing seasons while delivering exceptional potency and bag appeal. The strain develops its signature colors naturally as temperatures drop, making it perfect for outdoor cultivation in cooler climates. Indoor growers can encourage color development with slight temperature drops during late flowering.

The effects are deeply relaxing and euphoric, perfect for evening use or unwinding after a long day. Despite being indica-dominant, the initial cerebral buzz provides a pleasant mental uplift before settling into a comfortable body high that melts away stress and tension.

💜 Flavor Profile

Rich grape and berry flavors with sweet floral undertones. The terpene profile features high Myrcene for relaxation and Anthocyanins responsible for the beautiful purple colors.

🌙 Effects

Deeply relaxing and euphoric with initial cerebral uplift. Perfect for evening use, stress relief, and helping with sleep. Great for unwinding and meditation.
