About Noyes Haze

Noyes Haze represents the pinnacle of sativa breeding, combining legendary Original Haze genetics with the innovative Noyes foundation strains. This 80% sativa delivers the classic haze experience - soaring cerebral effects, incredible energy, and exceptional creativity enhancement - while incorporating modern improvements in yield, flowering time, and overall stability.

The strain exhibits classic haze characteristics with very tall growth, extensive branching, and beautiful light-green foliage that develops complex spicy and floral aromas during the extended flowering period. The Noyes genetics contribute enhanced resin production and slightly faster finishing times while maintaining the pure sativa effects that made haze legendary.

Effects are powerfully energizing and cerebral, perfect for daytime activities, creative projects, and social situations. The initial rush provides intense mental clarity and focus, followed by hours of sustained energy and euphoria. This is cannabis for the active mind - enhancing creativity, promoting deep conversations, and providing the motivation to tackle challenging projects with enthusiasm and clarity.
