About Alienoyes Kush

Alienoyes Kush represents the mysterious side of cannabis breeding, combining otherworldly Alien OG genetics with the signature Noyes foundation strains. This indica-dominant hybrid showcases the perfect marriage of alien genetics and traditional kush characteristics, creating a strain that's both familiar and uniquely exotic.

The strain develops with typical indica structure - medium height with dense branching and thick, resinous buds that take on an almost alien-green hue under proper lighting. The Alien OG contributes unique terpenes that create an unmistakable fuel and pine aroma, while the Noyes genetics add depth with earthy, spicy undertones that ground the experience.

Effects are deeply relaxing and introspective, perfect for evening use or meditation. The initial cerebral onset provides a spacey, contemplative high that slowly transitions into full-body relaxation. Users often report enhanced creativity and philosophical thinking, making this strain popular among artists and deep thinkers who appreciate its otherworldly mental effects combined with profound physical comfort.

🛸 Flavor Profile

Mysterious fuel and pine flavors with earthy kush undertones. Dominant Myrcene and Pinene create deep, complex aromatics with hints of spice and alien-like fuel notes.
