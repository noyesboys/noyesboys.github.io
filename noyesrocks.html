About Noyes Rocks

Noyes Rocks is a signature strain from Noyes Boys Genetics that combines their legendary Noyes Kush with the popular Biscotti strain. This indica-dominant hybrid delivers energizing effects that defy its indica lineage, providing motivation and happiness alongside the classic gas, earthy, and citrus flavor profile. The strain rocks with both potency and productivity, making it perfect for daytime use despite its 75% indica genetics.

The terpene profile is dominated by humulene and limonene, creating complex layers of gassy, earthy aromas with bright citrus highlights. Plants grow with a compact, short stature that makes them ideal for indoor cultivation or stealth outdoor grows. The dense, resinous buds showcase the quality breeding behind this Line 2 strain, with excellent bag appeal and a pungent aroma that demands proper storage.

Effects are uniquely energizing and motivating for an indica-dominant strain, providing clear-headed focus and happiness without the couch lock typically associated with indica genetics. Users report sustained energy, creativity, and productivity, making Noyes Rocks an excellent choice for daytime activities, work projects, or social situations. The high THC content ensures potent effects while the balanced cannabinoid profile maintains functional, uplifting highs.
