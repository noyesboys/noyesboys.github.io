Legend:

F1: First generation cross between two distinct parent strains.

F2: Second generation cross of F1 x F1.

IBL: Inbred line (typically F5 and beyond), e.g., Noyes Kush.

BX1: Backcrossed with one of its original parent strains for improved stability.

BX2+: Further backcrossed for even greater stability and reduced genetic drift.

Noyes Boys Genetic Line 3 - OG LINE

10 Noyes Boys Originals crossed with REAL OG strains sourced in Canada from old school growers. These classic genetics represent the authentic heritage of Canadian cannabis culture, bringing together time-tested genetics with our proven Noyes Boys foundation strains.
