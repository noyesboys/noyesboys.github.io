We Breed for Canadian Purples, Dense, Short, but well spaced internoding, all while looking for that perfect smell, profile and taste.
High Thc and good flavour is getting common these days, so we decided to breed for proper plant structure, mildew resistance and ease of growth for beginners, plant structure and resillience will always be our formost goal over just simply high thc or good smell, those should just come naturally if your working with the right genetics... like ours
