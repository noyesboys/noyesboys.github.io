About Super Noyes

Super Noyes represents an exciting collaboration between Noyes Boys Genetics' signature Noyes Kush and the popular Super Tangie strain. This indica-dominant hybrid combines the reliability and potency of Noyes Kush with the incredible fruity, citrus-forward terpene profile of Super Tangie. The result is a strain that delivers both exceptional bag appeal with its trichome-covered buds and an intoxicating fruity aroma that fills any grow space or room.

The strain expresses stunning visual characteristics with dense, heavily frosted buds that showcase exceptional trichome production. The Super Tangie influence brings vibrant orange hairs and a fruity, tangerine-dominant aroma that sets this strain apart from typical indica offerings. Plants grow with medium height and good structure, finishing in 68 days with generous yields of premium-quality flower that demands attention with its appearance and scent.

Effects lean indica with deep relaxation and stress relief, but the Super Tangie genetics add an uplifting, mood-enhancing quality that prevents overwhelming sedation. Users report a balanced high that starts with cerebral euphoria before transitioning into full-body relaxation. The fruity flavor profile makes every session enjoyable, while the high THC content and exceptional trichome coverage ensure potent, long-lasting effects perfect for evening use or unwinding after long days.
