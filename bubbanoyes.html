About Bubba Noyes

Bubba Noyes represents the perfect marriage of two legendary indica genetics - combining the classic sedating power and distinctive flavors of Bubba Kush with the bulletproof reliability and enhanced potency of Noyes Kush. This heavily indica-dominant strain delivers everything medical patients and indica lovers seek: profound relaxation, pain relief, sleep assistance, and that classic "couch-lock" experience that made Bubba Kush famous worldwide.

The Noyes genetics elevate the original Bubba Kush in every way - improved yields, enhanced mildew resistance, more consistent structure, and increased potency while maintaining all the beloved characteristics that made Bubba Kush a household name. The result is a strain that grows like a professional-grade variety but delivers the authentic Bubba experience that patients and recreational users have cherished for decades.

Effects are profoundly relaxing and deeply sedating, making Bubba Noyes perfect for evening use, chronic pain management, insomnia, anxiety, and stress relief. The heavy body stone provides therapeutic relief while the classic Bubba flavors - earthy, hashy, with hints of coffee and chocolate - create an authentic and enjoyable medicating experience. This is medical-grade cannabis at its finest, combining nostalgia with modern breeding excellence.
